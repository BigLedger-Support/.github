# BigLedger Support

Welcome to **BigLedger Support** — where BigLedger customers raise and track support requests
as GitHub issues, and our support assistant responds, keeps you updated, and follows through
to resolution.

## How support works here

- Each customer has dedicated **support repositories** (e.g. a **Finance** desk and an
  **Operations** desk), so the right people see the right requests.
- File a request as an **issue** using the templates (Problem / Question / Change / Data).
  **Screenshots, screen recordings, and voice notes are welcome** — we read images and listen to
  recordings in **English, Malay, Chinese, and Cantonese**.
- You'll get an acknowledgement, then progress updates (with due/target dates), then a resolution.
- **We never change your data** — our access is read-only; we extract, check, and explain, and
  schedule any system changes with our programmers.

## 🔐 Requesting access for yourself or a colleague

Access is granted per company. Your organisation has a **key person** (account administrator) who
manages who can use your support desks.

1. **If your company already has a desk:** ask your key person to add you. They can add anyone who
   already has a GitHub account to your company's support team.
2. **If you don't have a GitHub account yet:** create a free one at https://github.com/signup,
   then send your GitHub username (and which company/department — Finance or Operations) to your
   key person.
3. **New company / no key person yet, or not sure who that is:** email **support@bigledger.com**
   (or contact your BigLedger account manager) and we'll set it up.

> Why a key person? GitHub only lets organisation owners invite brand-new people by email, so each
> company nominates a key person we authorise to manage their own team going forward.
